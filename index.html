FAQ
Frequently
Asked Questions
Have questions? Our FAQ section has you covered with quick answers to the most common inquiries.
How long does it take to edit a video? +
Turnaround depends on video length and complexity. Most edits are delivered within 2–4 business days.
Can I request revisions? +
Yes! All packages come with revision rounds. We'll keep editing until you're fully satisfied.
What kind of footage should I provide? +
High-resolution clips (1080p or above), B-roll, logos, and any reference videos help us deliver your vision faster and better.
What if I don’t know which package to pick? +
No worries! You can book a free call and we’ll guide you to the best option based on your content goals.
How do I send you the footage? +
You can upload your files to any cloud service (Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer, etc.).
However, we prefer Google Drive because it's fast and easy. Just make sure to set the sharing permission to “Anyone with the link can view” so we can access your footage without delay.
